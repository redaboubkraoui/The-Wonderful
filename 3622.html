Meеt Kazоu, The Eуeless Cаt Whо Seеs With Нis Heаrt

Meet Kazou, a permanently blind cat who can’t see the beautiful world around him. The poor cat was found when his eyes were already badly damaged from the cat flu. Unfortunately, the condition of his eyes was so worse that they couldn’t be saved and had to be removed surgically. People think that this might stop Kazou from living his full life, however it is not right. This brave cat is living the life to its fullest even if he’s eyeless. Kazou is a very curious, cheeky and fearless cat. He loves to be outside his own garden and enjoy sunbathing on his stomach. Also, he likes chasing bumblebees and sensing them with his sense of smell and hearing. Everyone who has ever met Kazou is delighted and amazed by his behavior and his power. Despite a major handicap, he always tries to enjoy his full life and sees the world with his heart. We are all also certain that he can. So, we have collected some amazing photos of Kazou below.
